I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Section of Biostatistics at the Department of Public Health, University of Copenhagen.

You can contact me at name(dot)surname(at)sund(dot)ku(dot)dk.

My research interests include machine learning methods for time-to-event analysis of insurance and medical data, including settings with censoring and truncation, as well as statistical models for age-period-cohort data and claims development triangles.

I enjoy programming and developing open-source tools for practitioners and academics alongside my research.
